# The Negotiator 🐜 — Pest Control Edition

## What is this? (explain like I'm 5)

- You find pests in your kitchen — wasps, rats, bed bugs. You're grossed out and scared.
- You call the first exterminator you find. He sprays some cans.
- Bill: **€2,000** for what should've cost a few hundred.
- Our AI calls **several** exterminators at once, calm, fast, never grossed out.
- It compares prices, pushes back on fees, picks the fair one — before anyone shows up.

---

## The real problem

- Pests in your home feel urgent even when they're not life-or-death.
- Bait-and-switch trick: quote a cheap price on the phone → invent extra "special treatment" fees once they're inside your home → hand you a bill 5–10x higher.
- This isn't a one-off bad experience. **It's a documented, repeated scam pattern.**

**Real proof:**
- 🇩🇪 Germany: a woman found moths in her kitchen, exterminator sprayed 10 cans, billed her **~€2,000** — then charged her card **twice**
- 🇺🇸 USA: FTC deceptive-practice data shows initial quotes of ~$150 jumping to **$450 after treatment** in 25% of cases
- 🇺🇸 USA: real 2024 lawsuit (LA County v. Hawx Services) over bait-and-switch pest control contracts and hidden fees
- 🇺🇸 USA: hundreds of documented complaints (BBB, consumer reports) — same trick, different cities

---

## Why people don't shop around

- Disgust/fear overrides careful thinking — you want it gone **today**, not after 3 phone calls
- No time to compare — you just want someone in your house doing something about it
- No easy way to know what "fair" costs — every company quotes differently
- Companies **know this** and use the moment of panic to upsell

---

## Why this is a real business

- Fair price exists and is knowable — it's just never gathered fast, in the moment, by a stressed person
- Thousands of small operators, no big trusted brand
- Every one of them still **answers the phone** — that's the way in
- No grossed-out human is going to calmly call 4 companies and compare fee sheets. An AI will.

## How it makes money

- % of the money saved vs. the first quote you would've accepted
- Flat fee per successful negotiation
- Free basic price-check, paid for full "call and negotiate for me"
- Sell to property managers / landlords who deal with pest calls constantly

---

## What "real negotiation" looks like here

1. Agent calls Exterminator A → gets €600 quote for a wasp nest removal
2. Agent calls Exterminator B → **"I have a confirmed €250 quote for the same job — can you match it, all-in, no extra call-out fee?"**
3. Exterminator B drops to €280. Real price change, live, from real leverage.
4. Agent asks upfront: *"Is that the full price, or are there extra treatment fees added on-site?"* — catching the exact trick before anyone's in the house.
5. Every call ends with a clear number — never "he said it depends."
